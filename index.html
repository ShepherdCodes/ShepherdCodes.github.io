About Me

Rocketed out of Aerospace Engineering at SASTRA University, I now orbit the cosmos of Astrophysics at LMU Munich, chasing exoplanets, decoding star births, and crunching cosmic data like it’s space popcorn. Passionate about data-driven discovery and the secrets of the universe—basically, if it’s out there and twinkling, I probably have a spreadsheet on it.
